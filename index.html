How to Play

Keyboard Controls:

Arrow keys to control snake direction

Space or P key to pause/resume game

R key to quickly restart the game

Touch Controls (Mobile):

Swipe the screen to control snake direction

Use on-screen direction buttons to control snake

Game Rules:

Eat food to grow longer and earn points (10 points each)

Game over if you hit walls or your own body

Choose different difficulty levels (affects snake speed)

Game Tips:

Keep enough space between snake head and body

Plan your path ahead to avoid getting trapped
